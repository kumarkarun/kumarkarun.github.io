About Me

I am part of Comcast Applied AI Research group. I lead the Speech Machine Learning research at Comcast. My research and work focuses on speech processing, multi-modal learning and interpretability in deep learning.

Our work has won the Best Paper Award at ACL 2023.

I completed my MS in Computer Science from Syracuse University.
